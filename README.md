Quasim Ashiru (also known as Richolo) is a Cybersecurity Analyst, Penetration Tester, and Incident Responder from Lagos, Nigeria. He specializes in API security, web application security, mobile app security, and vulnerability assessment, and also works freelance, performing ethical hacking and responsible disclosure for organizations.

Quasim is skilled in web and API testing, reconnaissance, vulnerability enumeration, iOS app analysis, and security research. His toolkit includes Burp Suite, OWASP ZAP, Wireshark, iMazing, exiftool, and other industry-standard security tools. He also creates cybersecurity awareness content across LinkedIn, Facebook, and TikTok (@cyber_go_sec), teaching beginners and professionals how to understand modern cyber threats and apply security best practices.

He is currently pursuing a B.Sc in Computer Science at the National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN) and has earned certifications in Ethical Hacking (Alison), Cybersecurity & Pentesting (Digital World Tech Academy), and API Authentication (APIsec University).

Quasim is also the founder of Richolo_Official, an urban fashion brand. He is recognized for his practical, hands-on cybersecurity learning, responsible disclosure ethics, and his commitment to helping individuals and organizations improve their security posture.
